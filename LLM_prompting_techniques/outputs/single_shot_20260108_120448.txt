Thank you for reaching out to the IT Support Team. We are happy to provide you with a brief overview of what data science entails.

**Data science** is an interdisciplinary field that uses scientific methods, processes, algorithms, and systems to extract meaningful insights and knowledge from both structured and unstructured data. In simpler terms, it is the practice of transforming raw data into actionable strategies.

To give you a better understanding, data science typically involves a combination of the following disciplines:

*   **Mathematics and Statistics:** To analyze patterns and build predictive models.
*   **Computer Science/Programming:** Using languages like Python or R to manipulate data and automate processes.
*   **Domain Expertise:** Applying industry-specific knowledge to ensure the data is interpreted correctly within a business context.

Organizations use data science to identify trends, optimize operations, and make data-driven decisions that improve overall efficiency.
